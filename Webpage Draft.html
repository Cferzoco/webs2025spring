Getting Started

Here's some prompts to help you begin your journey through journaling!

Todays Date: __/__/__

Good Morning!

Here is where we will begin our day with a quick check in:

  • What are 3 goals you are going to prioritize today?
  • Clear your mind: What's the first thing on your mind this morning? How does this make you feel?
  • What is 1 thing you are going to do for

    yourself today?

Have a great day! We'll check in again tonight!

Hey Again! Tell me about your day?

  • Did you achieve your goals? If not, in what ways did you stay productive today?
  • Rate your stress levels on a scale of 1-10: What made you feel this way (good or bad)?
  • What was the best part of your day and why? how can we have more moments like this everyday?
